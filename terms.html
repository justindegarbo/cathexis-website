2. Description of Service

Cathexis is a body awareness and self-observation application that provides:

  • 35 therapeutic programs for specific concerns (anxiety, grief, trauma, chronic pain, postpartum, recovery, and more)
  • 4 starter programs and 25 single-day Life Event modules
  • 149 guided somatic interventions (breathing, grounding, movement techniques)
  • The Circle practice — a structured 10-step daily practice
  • Prediction tracking — comparing expected vs. actual experience
  • LINK pattern detection — AI-assisted pattern recognition across check-ins
  • Journal and reflection features
  • Progress tracking on original concerns
  • Therapist report export (PDF)
  • Educational content about neuroscience and embodied practices
